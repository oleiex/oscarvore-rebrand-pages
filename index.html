The channel works. It's just stuck in the wrong lane. People know you as the carnivore guy when you're actually about something bigger and more current — toxins, gut mechanism, and the body's barrier system. That's the wave. We're getting you on it before everyone else catches up.

04 //

Five pillars.

Every video on the main channel maps to one. If an idea doesn't fit a pillar, it doesn't get made.
PILLAR 01
Toxins
Heavy metals · oxidized oils · synthetic vitamins · endocrine disruptors. The lane dominating 2026 health conversation. Drives the "your supplement stack is a toxin stack" identity.
PILLAR 02
Mind + Meditation
Meditation · visualization · cortisol regulation · psychology of high performance. Elevated to co-equal pillar — cortisol is the biological bridge to hair, skin, hormones, gut. Mind work moves the same levers as body work.
PILLAR 03
Digestion Mechanism
Zonulin · LPS · tight junctions · MMC · motility. The mechanism stack that explains everything downstream. Highest share-rate pillar.
PILLAR 04
Testosterone
Sharper than "Hormones" — gives the pillar a name people search for. Content still covers the broader hormone cascade (cortisol · thyroid · estrogen) but anchors around testosterone for SEO + recognition.
PILLAR 05
Lifestyle
The lane that's uniquely yours. City tier-lists (Dubai, Bali — already winners on the channel), UFC consult deep-dives, private-client case studies, tribe research as authority anchors. Broad appeal, attracts the high-performer audience as warm leads.
The Through-Line · How To Bounce Between Topics Smoothly
Every video, regardless of pillar, runs the same 3-beat structure: problem → mechanism → protocol. That structural consistency is what lets one channel cover toxins on Monday and meditation on Friday and still feel like one creator, one philosophy. The audience trusts the framework — they follow you across topics. Reference: Teaching Men's Fashion (~5M subs) uses the same playbook — fashion · fitness · dating · mindset, all under a self-improvement umbrella. The umbrella is the brand. The pillars are the surface area.
Weekly Output Mix
2× Toxins · 2× Mind + Meditation · 1× Digestion · 1× Testosterone · 2× Lifestyle = 8 shorts/week. Mind/Meditation co-equal with the physical pillars per Oscar's call. Plus 1 main-channel long-form per week + 1 corporate-channel client interview every 2-3 weeks.
